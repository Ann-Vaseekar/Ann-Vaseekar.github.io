Spooky fun fact of the day!

One theory for the term Jack-O'-Lantern traces its roots back to an Irish folktale. A man named Stingy Jack tricked the devil, and when he died, he was not allowed into either heaven or hell. Instead, his soul was doomed to wander the earth with a lit turnip to guide him. The Irish referred to this ghostly figure as Jack of the Lantern, which eventually became Jack-O'-Lantern. This doesn't explain where the pumpkin came from, but it's a fun fact nonetheless.
